About Us

Welcome to Yujan Pharmacy, your trusted destination for comprehensive mental health care in Butwal, Rupandehi. We are more than just a pharmacy; we are your dedicated partners on the journey to mental health well-being. At Yujan Pharmacy, we understand that mental health is just as important as physical health, and we are committed to providing the highest quality care and support for individuals facing mental health challenges.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer a holistic approach to mental health care, emphasizing the importance of compassion, understanding, and evidence-based treatments. We believe that everyone deserves to lead a fulfilling and emotionally healthy life, and we are here to make that vision a reality.
